- Frequently Asked Questions -

1. How do I create an account? ➕
Click "Sign Up," enter your email and password, then confirm your email. ✅
2. How can I change my password? ➕
Go to "Settings" → "Change Password" and follow the steps. 🔒
3. How do I delete my account? ➕
Visit account settings and select "Delete Account." ❌
4. How can I download the mobile app? ➕
Find us on Google Play or the App Store by searching for "[App Name]." 📲
5. How do I contact support? ➕
Send us a message via "Contact Us" or email support@example.com. 📨
6. How do I reset a forgotten password? ➕
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions. 🔁
7. How do I enable dark mode? ➕
Go to "Settings" and toggle "Dark Mode" on. 🌙
8. How do I change the website language? ➕
In "Settings," select your preferred language from the dropdown. 🌍
9. How do I unsubscribe from email notifications? ➕
Disable "Receive Notifications" in your profile settings. 📩❌
10. How can I report a user? ➕
Click "Report" on their profile and describe the issue. 🚨
